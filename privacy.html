Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

No data collected. No servers. 100% offline.

Overview

DinkIQ is built with privacy as a core principle. The app works entirely offline — your data never leaves your device. We do not operate servers, collect analytics, or sell data of any kind.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Your match history or performance data
  • Location information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

Data Stored on Your Device

All match data you log is stored locally in an SQLite database on your device. This data is never transmitted anywhere. Deleting the app permanently removes all stored data.

In-App Purchases

DinkIQ Pro purchases are processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We use RevenueCat to verify your purchase status — this communicates with Apple's servers using your Apple ID purchase receipt. RevenueCat does not receive or store your personal data beyond a random anonymous customer ID. For more, see RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Photo Library

When you share a match card, the app captures a screenshot and opens iOS's native share sheet. We do not automatically save anything to your photo library unless you explicitly choose to save it.

Third-Party Services

DinkIQ uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — payment processing
  • RevenueCat — purchase verification (anonymous)

No advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or analytics services are used.

Children's Privacy

DinkIQ does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no data collection mechanisms of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with a new "Last Updated" date.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at support@dinkiq.app.
